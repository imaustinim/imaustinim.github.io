Hello, it's nice to meet you.
Initially, I wasn't planning on writing anything but I wanted to show a side of myself that may not be as apparent at first glance. The first thing you should know about me is that I love life; I love living - everything, the good, the bad, it's all a journey and we just once shot at it.

I'm passionate, extremely versatile, and really value the importance of effort. My unique background allows me to make objective judgements from various perspectives to create wholistic programs with the end user in mind. I pride myself on my ability to discern under pressure and to adapt to any given situation.

I wanted to pursue a career where I could help others by creating products that provide real, economic value. Now, being a software developer, I'm able to utilize my entire skillset to exponentially increase my impact and create value.
Outside of work...
Honestly, I'm a simple man and I enjoy the simple things in life. A good song, a cold beer, and friends and family. I love going on roadtrips, and backpacking when I can. When I'm travelling, I like to do as the locals do and take everything in. My most memorable experience so far was in Vietnam when I biked from Da Nang to Hanoi by myself; I almost died multiple times.
Where I've been: Cambodia, Ghana, Greece, India, Italy, Mexico, South Korea, Thailand, Vietnam
Movies, and stories in general, have always had a impact on me. I've actually had the opportunity to produce a film back in 2018 called Table For Three.
Favourite Movies: Interstellar, The Dark Knight, Parasite, So Long My Son

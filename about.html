Hello, it's nice to meet you.
The first thing you should know about me is that I love life; the good, the bad. It's all an amazing journey and we get one shot at it.
I'm passionate, extremely versatile, and I deeply value the importance of effort. I studied medical sciences, I've been a teacher, an event mananger, started my own record label, and worked in finance for the past couple of years. And after years of searching, I can say confidently, "software development is home for me." My unique background allows me to make sound judgements from various perspectives to create holistic programs with the end user in mind. I pride myself on my ability to discern under pressure and to adapt to any given situation.
I'm pursuing a career where I can help others by creating products that provide real, economic value. As a software developer, I'm able to use my entire skillset to exponentially increase my impact and create value.
